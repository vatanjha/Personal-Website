Hello! I am Vatan Jha, a researcher and mathematics enthusiast with a strong interest in mathematical modeling, dynamical systems, and computational analysis. My work primarily focuses on the modeling and simulation of calcium dynamics in biological systems, including stability analysis, bifurcation theory, and fuzzy mathematical approaches. I enjoy combining mathematics, computation, and biological applications to better understand complex nonlinear systems. Through my research, presentations, and academic work, I aim to contribute to the advancement of computational science and applied mathematics while continuously learning and exploring new ideas.
